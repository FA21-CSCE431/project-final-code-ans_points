ANSPoints User Manual

If you are an American Nuclear Society member at Texas A&M University you are at the right place!

This document will guide you through using the ANS Points application so you can earn points while participating in all the great events that ANS provides every semester.

Where can I find the ANS Points website?

Visit anspoints-staging.herokuapp.com

Attending an ANS event? Get your points!

QR Code

Scan the QR code that an ANS officer will display at the meeting.

Enter your TAMU email, first name, and last name, and click Check In. You're done!

Event Code

If a QR code is not being displayed at the meeting, or you are attending the meeting remotely, don’t worry, if you are given the event code, you can enter it instead! Navigate to the events page (the homepage), find the ongoing event you are attending, click join, and enter the event code.

Enter your TAMU email, first name, and last name (same form as above from the QR code). You're done!

Looking for an upcoming ANS event?

Navigate to the events page and scroll to Upcoming Events. Here you can see what events you can attend to get points in the future!

Want to check how many points you have?

Navigate to the points page. Enter your TAMU email in the search bar and click Search. This will show your email (if you have previously signed in to an event) and the number of points you currently have! You can also search your friends' emails and if they have fewer points, tell them to pick up the slack!

Want to contact an ANS officer or an event speaker?

Navigate to the contacts page. The contact cards shown are the information of the officers, speakers, or others that ANS officers have provided you to contact.

Missing points?

If you are missing point(s) that you believe you should have received, contact an ANS officer at americannuclearsociety.tamu@gmail.com or https://ans-tamu.weebly.com/contact.html and they can manually enter your point(s) if your request is validated.
